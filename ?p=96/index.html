Marty and Curt appearing at Midwest Gaming Classic

Marty Goldberg will be appearing in person at the Midwest Gaming Classic in Milwaukee, Wisconsin on March 23, joined by Curt Vendel live via Skype. The two will be discussing the making of Atari Inc. – Business Is Fun and will be joined in the last section by legendary pinball designer Steve Ritchie, who started his career in Atari’s Pinball Division. Just after the talk, Marty will also be selling and signing copies at a reduced show price.

This will be followed by a live Skype interview with Atari coin-op game designer Owen Rubin on March 24.
